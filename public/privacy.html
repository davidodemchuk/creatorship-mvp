Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. What We Collect from Creators

When you connect your TikTok account via our platform, we collect:

  • Profile data: display name, avatar URL, follower count, video count
  • Public videos: video list, titles, view counts, engagement metrics
  • Access token: OAuth token to read your public profile and video list (we never post on your behalf)

We use this data solely to match your content with brand advertising opportunities and to enable payouts.

2. What We Collect from Brands

When you sign up or use the brand portal, we collect:

  • Account data: email, store name, brand name, password (hashed)
  • Meta credentials: ad account ID, access token, page ID (when you launch campaigns) — used only to create and manage Meta ads

3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to: operate the Creatorship platform; match creator content with brands; process payouts; run Meta ad campaigns on behalf of brands; improve our services; and comply with legal obligations.

4. What We Never Do

We never sell your data. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

We never post to TikTok. Our access to TikTok is read-only. We never create, edit, or publish content on your behalf.

5. Third Parties

We share data with the following service providers necessary to operate Creatorship:

  • TikTok API — to authenticate and read your public profile and videos
  • Meta API — to create and manage ad campaigns on behalf of brands
  • Stripe — to process creator payouts and brand payments
  • Railway — for hosting and infrastructure

Each provider has its own privacy policy governing their use of data.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and secure storage of credentials. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. We do not store plaintext passwords.

7. Your Rights

You may: access the data we hold about you; request correction of inaccurate data; request deletion of your account and associated data; and opt out of marketing communications. Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Deletion

To delete your account and associated data, email us at the address below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days, except where we must retain data for legal or contractual reasons.

9. CCPA (California)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to: know what personal information we collect; request deletion of your personal information; opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information); and non-discrimination for exercising these rights.
